Our Story

We Are w'ink ai

Born from a simple belief: every Indian brand deserves luxury-grade visuals. No studio required. One prompt ahead.

Founded 2026

The Idea That
Changed Everything

It started with a single observation: a jewellery seller in Mumbai was losing sales to bigger brands — not because her products were inferior, but because her photos looked cheap. Her rings were exquisite. Her photography was not.

We asked ourselves: what if AI could give every small brand the same visual power as the biggest luxury houses in the world? What if a cake shop in Jaipur could have visuals indistinguishable from a Michelin-starred restaurant in Paris?

"Luxury is not a budget. It is a mindset — and now, it is a technology."

w'ink ai was built to democratise premium visual marketing. We trained our AI specifically on luxury brand aesthetics — not generic photography, but the specific lighting, composition and depth that separates a ₹500 product photo from a ₹50,000 one.
